# **App Name**: Gentlecut Guild

## Core Features:

- Service Catalog & Pricing: Display a comprehensive list of all barber services offered, complete with detailed descriptions, durations, and transparent pricing. Services are categorized for easy navigation.
- Appointment Booking System: Allow customers to view available slots for specific services and barbers, select a date and time, and book their appointment. Users receive a confirmation upon successful booking.
- Barber Profiles & Portfolios: Showcase individual barber profiles, including their specialties, years of experience, and a portfolio of their work, allowing clients to choose their preferred professional.
- AI Style Recommender Tool: An AI-powered tool that suggests potential hairstyles and beard styles to customers based on various inputs, such as face shape, hair type, and desired look.
- User Account Dashboard: Provide a personalized dashboard for logged-in users to manage their upcoming appointments, view booking history, and update personal information.
- Responsive Design & Navigation: Ensure the application provides an optimal viewing and interaction experience across all devices, from desktops to mobile phones, with intuitive navigation.
- Gallery of Work: A visual gallery showcasing high-quality photos of finished haircuts, shaves, and the overall salon ambiance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary accent color: A rich, sophisticated red (#CF1717) to convey passion, energy, and classic barbershop allure.
- Background color: A very subtle off-white (#F5F0F0), almost white, providing a clean and bright foundation that highlights the primary and secondary colors.
- Secondary accent color: A deep, elegant blue (#2258C3) to provide a contrasting yet complementary tone, reminiscent of traditional barbershop poles.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for a high-end, elegant, and classic feel, enhancing the sophisticated brand identity.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for excellent readability and a modern touch, ensuring all textual content is clear and accessible.
- Utilize clean, vector-based icons that evoke classic barbershop elements like scissors, combs, and razors, designed with modern simplicity.
- Employ a modern, spacious layout with clear content hierarchy and ample white space. The design must be fully responsive, adapting seamlessly to screens of all sizes to maintain elegance and usability on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
- Implement subtle, smooth transitions and hover effects for interactive elements, enhancing the user experience without being distracting. Focus on fluid animations for navigation and booking interactions.
